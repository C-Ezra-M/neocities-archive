## What is FIRE?
<dfn>FIRE</dfn> (short for <dfn>Fight in Reshiram's Earth</dfn>) was a camp running from 2021 and 2024, hosted solely by C.Ezra.M, though in some challenges guest judges were invited. The contestants played as Pokémon. It is C.Ezra.M's first completed camp. For this reason, he made it this small.
