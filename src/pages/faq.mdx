        # FAQ
        ## What does C.Ezra.M stand for?
        While it really stands for "Cody Ezra Marcel" (which is also a nickname, yet C.Ezra.M is a short version of it), it comes from my last name backwards. Yes, my last name sounds just like the month of March in Polish.

        ## How to contact you?
        I am on [Discord](https://discord.gg/cNu3zpr), [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@C.Ezra.M), and [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/c-ezra-m.bsky.social).

        ## How did you make this website?
        With [Astro](https://astro.build/) and [MDX](https://mdxjs.com/)! For styling, I used [Sass](https://sass-lang.com/), an extension language that compiles to CSS.

        ## What conlangs are you interested in?
        My main ones as of September 2025 seem to be [Toki Pona](https://tokipona.org/) and [Lojban](https://lojban.io/), the latter mostly because of la janbe's songs! I also want to learn [Globasa](https://www.globasa.net/).

        ### How did you learn Toki Pona?
        I joined [ma pona pi toki pona](https://discord.gg/mapona) and used [jan Sa's cheatsheet](https://jansa-tp.github.io/sheets/English/Toki%20Pona%20Cheat%20Sheet%20v2.pdf).
        
        I started in late March 2025. At that time, [Wasona](https://wasona.com/) didn't exist, but I heavily recommend it. This course by kala Asi includes sitelen pona throughout, which helps in memorizing words! Myself, I [translated it into Polish](https://wasona.com/pl/).
