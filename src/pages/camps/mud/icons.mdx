        # Mutually Unhinged Destruction contestant icon inspirations
        ## Why did I make them?

        Because not all contestants were in BFB, which would prompt the creation of those icons (some of those contestants were TPOT debut candidates or BFDI Mini contestants). I decided to make icons for all contestants for the sake of a consistent style.

        ## The list
