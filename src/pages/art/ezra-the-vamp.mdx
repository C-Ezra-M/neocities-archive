My OC, Ezra, as a vampire

The process was as follows:
- I took the layers I used for my other PFP, but I treated the character as one layer
- I took them into GIMP and restricted the colors to a 16-color palette (as in CGA, an old IBM graphics card)
- I made some edits, which mostly come down to changing some colors
- And there I have it!
