title: What if BFDI was a contestant vote?

To this day, BFDI holds up the practice of allowing the viewers to choose who will be eliminated next episode. This may have been influenced by *Big Brother*, where the audience can vote via telephone.

Inanimate Insanity has practiced this in the past, but they have later introduced contestant-only voting. Let's see how BFDI would go if only the contestants could vote.

## Voting Rules

- During the team phase, the ones on the losing team are the ones voting.
- During the merge, everyone who is still in can vote.
- Win Tokens can still be used as normal.
